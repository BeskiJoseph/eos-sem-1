Return Policy

At Trendy Closet, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return the product within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange. Please ensure that the item is in its original condition, unworn and unwashed.

Steps to Return an Item:
  1. Contact our customer service to initiate the return process.
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging.
  3. Include a copy of the original invoice.
  4. Ship the item back to us using a trackable shipping method.
